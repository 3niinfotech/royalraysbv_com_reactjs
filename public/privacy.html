Privacy Policy

Royal Rays BV (“Royal Rays”, “We”, “Us”) is committed to complying with applicable privacy laws. Protecting your personal data is important to us. This Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, who we may share it with, and how you can control it.

What information we collect

Information you provide: name, contact number, postal address, email, username, password, and other details when you register or use services.

Information collected automatically:

  • IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Pages and links you visit, ads you view

How we use your information

We use information to provide services, customize content, send updates (with consent), produce anonymized statistics, improve products, and publish reviews you make public.

Disclosure of information

We may share data with service providers who assist us (maintenance, analytics, marketing, etc.). They are permitted to use your data only to perform tasks for us and must keep it confidential. We may also disclose data to comply with law or to protect rights and safety.

Your choices

You can opt out of promotional messages via the unsubscribe link in communications or through account settings in the Application.

Security

We implement reasonable physical and technical safeguards. However, no system is completely secure.

Links to other sites

Our Application may link to other sites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please check their policies.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. If we make material changes, we'll post a notice in the Application and update the effective date.
